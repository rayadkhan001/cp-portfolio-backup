Magbook

Project Details

  • Category : Web Developmet
  • Client : Olivia Samuel
  • Images by : Matthew Grace

Magbook is a news portal website developed using WordPress. This content management system is tailored to meet the demands of a news-centric platform. It enables the easy creation and publication of news articles, multimedia content, and interactive features, all presented in an organized and visually appealing layout. Through WordPress, Magbook efficiently manages news content and offers a user-friendly experience to its readers.
